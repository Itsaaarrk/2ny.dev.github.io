Delete Account and Data — HMS Medical

Developer: 2ny — listed on Google Play as 2ny .

How to request deletion: Use the form below or send an email to mxaidocn@gmail.com. This page documents the steps we require, the data we will delete or retain, and expected processing times.

1. Request deletion

Option A — Email request (recommended)

  1. Send an email to mxaidocn@gmail.com with subject: Delete my HMS Medical account.
  2. Include the following information in the email: app username or account ID, email address registered with the account, and a short statement that you request permanent deletion of your account and associated data.
  3. We will reply to the registered email to verify your identity before processing the deletion.

2. Verification

To protect accounts, we will verify the request by sending a confirmation link to the email address registered with the account or by asking for a short verification code. We will not process deletion requests from unverified addresses.

3. What will be deleted

  • Account profile: username, display name, profile photo.
  • User‑provided content: posts, messages, uploaded files (unless otherwise stated below).
  • Personal identifiers: email address, phone number, billing identifiers (if any) — removed from active systems.
  • Device identifiers and analytics tied to the account: removed or anonymized where feasible.

4. What may be retained and why

  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics: retained for product improvement; cannot be used to identify you.
  • Transaction records: payment or billing records may be retained for legal, tax, or fraud‑prevention reasons for up to 7 years (or local legal requirement).
  • Backups: deleted data may persist in backups for up to 30 days before permanent removal.

5. Retention and processing time

We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days. After verification, account deletion will be completed within 30 days except for data retained for legal or compliance reasons (see section 4). Backups may be purged within an additional 30 days.

6. Additional notes

  • If you request deletion, you may lose access to purchases, subscriptions, or content that cannot be restored.
  • If you want a copy of your data before deletion, request a data export in your deletion email; we will provide an export within 14 days where feasible.
  • If your account is linked to third‑party services (e.g., social login), you may need to revoke access from those services separately.

7. Contact & appeals

Contact: mxaidocn@gmail.com. If you believe deletion was performed in error, contact us within 30 days and we will investigate.
